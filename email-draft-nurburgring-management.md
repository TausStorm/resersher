## Our experience as customers

We are a group of 4 friends flying to Germany specifically to celebrate at the Nürburgring. We want to spend money — on track laps, experiences, activities, food, hotels. But after two weeks of research, here's what we've been through:

**1. Read the website** — Couldn't figure out how anything works.
**2. Googled extensively** — Found contradicting or outdated info.
**3. Used AI research tools** — Even AI couldn't extract clear answers from your site.
**4. Contacted customer service** — Was told "come here and we'll figure it out on-site."

For a trip we're flying internationally to attend, "just show up and see" is not acceptable. We want to plan. We want to know what we're doing before we get there.

## Specific website problems

### The language issue
When you switch to English, approximately 50% of the site remains in German. Menu items, descriptions, FAQ content, booking flows — mixed languages throughout. For an international destination that attracts visitors from all over Europe and the world, this is a fundamental failure.

### No pricing information
I created an account. I found that I can top up my balance with euros. But **nowhere on the entire website does it state what a single lap costs.** How much per lap on the Nordschleife? How much on the GP track? This information simply doesn't exist on the site. A visitor has to blindly load money and hope for the best.

### Impossible navigation
The site has deeply nested menus with dozens of items. Finding something specific requires clicking through 4-5 levels. There's no clear "visitor guide" or "plan your visit" page that answers the basic questions:
- What can I do here?
- What does it cost?
- How do I book it?
- When is it available?

### Activities can't be pre-booked
- **Ring°Kartbahn:** "Slot tickets can only be bought on site on the same day." Why? This creates anxiety for visitors who don't know if they'll get a slot.
- **Offroad Experience:** Listed publicly on the website but turns out to be B2B-only. This is nowhere indicated on the site.
- **Caterham Drift Experience:** Has a page but no clear booking flow or pricing.
- **Tourist drives:** The entire credit system is unexplained — no pricing, no clear process.

### FAQ is empty
The FAQ page (nuerburgring.de/info/service/faq) says "click your way through" — but all the content is hidden behind JavaScript accordions that don't render for many visitors, and even when they do, the answers are vague.

### Customer service response
Your team (Pascale was friendly and helpful!) essentially told us: come to the info°center when you arrive and we'll help you. For a trip we're planning months in advance, spending thousands of euros on flights, hotels, rental cars — we need answers before we arrive, not after.

## What you're losing

Every person who gives up trying to understand your website either:
- Doesn't come at all
- Comes but spends less because they couldn't plan
- Goes to third-party providers (RSR Nürburg, Rent4Ring, etc.) who have clear websites with clear pricing

I guarantee you are losing significant revenue from international visitors who simply cannot navigate the booking process.
