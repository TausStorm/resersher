# Iran Naval Blockade — Investment Thesis

**Scenario:** US naval blockade removes ~1.5-2M bbl/day Iranian oil from global markets. Buyers (primarily China, India) forced to find alternative supply. US oil, gas, and LNG become the swing supplier.

**This is a thought experiment / speculative analysis. Not financial advice.**

---

## THE BIG PICTURE

- Iran = ~2% of global oil supply. Removing it = meaningful tightening
- Historical precedent: 2012 Iran sanctions → oil spiked 25%. 2018 → 30%. 1979 → 120%
- Brent already hit $119.50 intraday in March 2026
- **Base case: oil sustains $90-110/bbl.** Escalation case: $130+
- Brent-WTI spread blew out to ~$25/bbl → US-based producers/refiners have a massive structural cost advantage
- USD strengthens (US is now net exporter — higher oil prices *help* US trade balance while hurting importers)

## RISK FACTORS — WHAT KILLS THE THESIS

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| **Blockade lifted / peace deal** | Medium | High — energy stocks drop 15-25% fast | Use stop-losses. Don't go all-in at once. |
| **OPEC+ floods market** | Low-Medium | High — Saudi has 3-5M bbl/day spare capacity | They prefer to raise slowly to maximize revenue. Watch OPEC+ meetings. |
| **Global recession** | Medium | High — demand destruction offsets supply loss | Gold (GLD) and USD (UUP) hedge this. |
| **China retaliation** | Medium | Medium — trade war escalation, tariffs | Diversify beyond pure oil plays. |
| **Energy stocks already priced in** | High | Medium — stocks up 20%+ YTD, insiders selling $1.4B in Q1 | Wait for pullbacks. Scale in gradually. |
| **Renewables acceleration** | Low (short-term) | Low — takes years to build alternatives | This is a 1-3 year thesis, not 10-year. |

### The Biggest Risk Nobody Talks About
Energy executives themselves sold $1.4B of their own stock in Q1 2026. When insiders sell at this pace, they may think prices are peaking. Don't chase. Scale in on pullbacks.

## TIMING / EXECUTION

1. **Don't FOMO in.** Energy stocks already up 20%+ YTD. Wait for a 5-10% pullback.
2. **Scale in gradually.** Deploy capital over 4-8 weeks, not all at once.
3. **Set stop-losses.** If blockade is lifted, you want automatic protection. 15-20% trailing stops.
4. **Take profits on the way up.** This is a geopolitical trade, not a forever hold. Sell 25% at +30%, another 25% at +50%.
5. **Time horizon:** 6-18 months. This isn't a decade-long thesis.
6. **Position sizing:** No more than 20-30% of total portfolio in this theme. Geopolitical trades can reverse fast.
