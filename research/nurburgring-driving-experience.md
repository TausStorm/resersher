# Nürburgring Driving Experience Research

*Research date: April 2026 | Target dates: Late June / Early July 2026*

---

## Overview

The Nürburgring offers multiple ways to drive:
1. **Touristenfahrten** - drive your own (or rented) car on the Nordschleife
2. **RentRaceCar** - rent a prepared race car and drive with a coach
3. **Co-Pilot / RaceTaxi** - ride shotgun with a pro racing driver
4. **Nürburgring Driving Academy** - Formula 4 and sports driver training
5. **Lamborghini rental** - via third-party providers (RSR Nürburg, Apex, etc.)

---

## 1. Touristenfahrten (Tourist Drives)

### What is it?
The Nordschleife opens as a "public road" for anyone to drive their own car or motorbike. No booking required - just buy credit and go.

### Schedule (current example from official site)
- **Nordschleife:** 17:30 - 19:30 (typical weekday, ~2 hrs)
- **Grand Prix Track:** 18:00 - 20:00

**Important:** In summer (June/July), sessions are longer and may include earlier morning sessions on weekends. Check the official schedule: https://nuerburgring.de/driving/touristdrives

### Pricing
- Credit-based system - buy online or on-site
- Typical lap price: ~30 EUR per lap (Nordschleife, 20.8 km)
- Register for an account for bonus system / discounts

### Key facts
- No appointment needed
- Track can close anytime (weather, accidents)
- Normal traffic rules apply (StVO)
- Overtake only on the LEFT
- Mixed traffic - motorcycles to tour buses
- No speed limit (but recommended to drive carefully)
- 20.8 km, 73 corners, 300m elevation difference

### URL
https://nuerburgring.de/driving/touristdrives

**Website:** https://nuerburgring.de/driving/driving-academy/formula-trainings

**YOU drive a real Formula 4 car** on the Grand Prix Track!

## Late June / Early July Availability

- **Touristenfahrten:** Open almost daily in summer. Longer hours (sometimes morning + evening sessions). Best season.
- **RentRaceCar:** 23 days available in June, 24 in July. Book early for weekends.
- **Formula Training:** Check specific dates on Nürburgring site (select "Dates" in product dropdown)
- **RaceTaxi:** Available ~170 days/year, very likely available in June/July

### Watch out for:
- **NLS race weekends** (Nürburgring Langstrecken-Serie) - track closed for Touristenfahrten during races, BUT you can book NLS co-pilot (649 EUR) to ride during Friday practice
- **Rock am Ring** (music festival) - typically early June, may affect access
- Check event calendar: https://nuerburgring.de/events
