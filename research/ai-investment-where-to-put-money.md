# AI Boom — Where to Actually Invest

Everyone owns NVIDIA. The smart money is in the stuff AI NEEDS to run — power, cooling, buildings, cables. The "picks and shovels" of the AI gold rush.

---

## THE KEY INSIGHT

**Power is the bottleneck, not chips.**

- A single AI data center uses as much electricity as a small city (500MW-1GW)
- US data centers need **32-52 GW of new power capacity by 2030** (Goldman Sachs)
- Lead time to build new power generation: **3-15 years**
- Every hyperscaler CEO says the same thing: "We can't get enough power"
- Microsoft restarted Three Mile Island nuclear plant. Amazon bought a nuclear-powered data center. Google signed nuclear deals.

Software scales infinitely. Electricity doesn't. **Invest in what can't be copy-pasted.**

---

## THE AI VALUE CHAIN — Where the Money Flows

```
You pay for ChatGPT/AI
    ↓
Cloud providers (AWS, Azure, Google) take your money
    ↓
They buy chips (NVIDIA, AMD, custom ASICs)
    ↓
Chips are made by TSMC (monopoly)
    ↓
Chips need HBM memory (Micron, SK Hynix)
    ↓
Chips sit in servers in DATA CENTERS (Equinix, Digital Realty)
    ↓
Data centers need NETWORKING (Arista, Broadcom)
    ↓
Data centers need COOLING (Vertiv, Modine)
    ↓
Data centers need ELECTRICITY (Constellation, Vistra, GE Vernova)
    ↓
Electricity needs FUEL (uranium, natural gas)
    ↓
Everything needs to be BUILT (Quanta, Corning, Eaton)
```

**The further down this chain you go, the less "priced for AI" the stocks are.**

## THE CONTRARIAN VIEW — Why Power Is Smarter Than Chips

Everyone is buying NVIDIA, AMD, and Broadcom. They're priced at 40-70x earnings assuming AI growth continues perfectly.

But here's the thing:
- **Chip companies compete with each other.** NVIDIA vs AMD vs custom ASICs vs whatever comes next.
- **Power companies don't compete with physics.** You need electricity. Period. There's no "software update" that eliminates the need for power.
- **Data centers don't compete with geography.** Northern Virginia has 70% of the world's internet traffic. Dominion Energy serves it. That doesn't change.
- **Cooling doesn't get disrupted.** Chips generate heat. Heat needs removal. Vertiv removes it.

The AI chip winner might change. The need for power, cooling, fiber, and buildings never changes.
