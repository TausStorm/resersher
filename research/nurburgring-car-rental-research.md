### 4-Person Suitability
Most RSR cars are 2-seaters (Porsches, Corvette) or 2+2 (BMW M2/M4). **Best 4-person options:**
- VW Golf R (mk8) — full 4-seater, 4 laps from €350
- BMW M240i xDrive — tight rear seats, 4 laps from €722
- Hyundai i30N — full 4-seater, 2 laps from €270
- Toyota GR Yaris — technically 4-seat, small rear

### Multi-Day Rental
RSR primarily operates on a **per-session / per-lap basis**, not daily rental. You book laps or session blocks. For multi-day, you'd book multiple sessions across different Touristenfahrten days. Contact them directly for multi-day packages.

### Track Insurance & Excess (RSR)
- Cars are **insured for road and track** (Nordschleife TF + GP track)
- **You pay for damage up to the excess maximum**
- Excess ranges:
  - Low-end cars: **€15,000**
  - Mid-range: **€35,000**
  - High-end: **€50,000**
  - Supercars: **€200,000**
- Road (road tours): €5,000 excess; high-end €12,500
- **Excess reduction available** via Gullwing Insurance partnership (extra fee per day)
  - Example: Hyundai i30N excess reduces from €25,000 → €12,000 for €876
- Third-party excess on TF: €10,000 per claim
- Deposits: €1,000 - €20,000 possible (supercars ~10% of car value)
- **Mandatory safety briefing** before driving
- Helmets mandatory on track

### Recommended Track Insurance Providers
- **trackdayinsurance.com** — promo code: RSR2026 (covers cars up to €150,000-€300,000)
- **Gullwing Racing Insurance** (NL): https://gullwing.com/en/race/

## 5. Frankfurt Airport — Luxury / Sports Car Rental

Frankfurt (FRA) is ~1.5 hours from the Nürburgring. Best options for picking up a luxury/sports car:

### SIXT Luxury (Frankfurt Airport)
- **Website:** https://www.sixt.com/luxury-cars/germany/frankfurt/
- **Location:** Frankfurt Airport (FRA) — major branch
- **Fleet:** BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Audi, Porsche, Range Rover, Volvo
- **Models typically available:** BMW 5/7 Series, Mercedes E/S Class, Audi A6/A8, Porsche Cayenne/Panamera, BMW X5/X7
- **Sports car availability:** Porsche 911, BMW M models sometimes available in "luxury" or "sports" category — but NOT guaranteed
- **Pricing estimate (3-4 days):**
  - BMW 5 Series / Mercedes E-Class: ~€300-500 total
  - Porsche Cayenne / BMW X5: ~€500-800 total
  - Porsche 911 / BMW M4 (if available): ~€800-1,500 total
- **Track eligibility: NO** — standard rental T&C explicitly prohibit racetrack use. Insurance void on any track.
- **Minimum age:** 25 for luxury categories
- **Book:** https://www.sixt.com

### Europcar Prestige (Frankfurt Airport)
- **Website:** https://www.europcar.de
- **Location:** Frankfurt Airport
- **Fleet:** Mercedes, BMW, Audi premium models
- **Track eligibility: NO**
- **Pricing:** Similar to SIXT

### Enterprise Exotic (Germany)
- Enterprise/National does not operate a dedicated "exotic" division in Germany like in the US
- Standard premium fleet (Mercedes, BMW, Audi) available at airports
- **Track eligibility: NO**

### Specialty Luxury Rental (deliver to Frankfurt area)
Several German specialty companies offer supercar rental with delivery:
- **Luxury & Services** (luxuryandservices.de) — Lamborghini, Ferrari, Porsche, McLaren rental
- **MVP Rent** — Lamborghini, Ferrari rental
- **Drivar** (drivar.de) — Supercar rental throughout Germany
- These are road-only; **NO track use** permitted

## 8. Recommended Strategy for 4 People + Track Use

### Option A: RSR Nürburg + Airport Car (Best combo)
1. **Rent a "normal" car from SIXT/Europcar at CGN or FRA** for road transport (€200-400 for 3-4 days)
2. **Book RSR track sessions** at the Nürburgring for track driving
3. Best RSR 4-seater for track: **VW Golf R mk8** (4 laps from €350) or **Hyundai i30N** (2 laps from €270)
4. Total budget: ~€600-1,500 depending on how many laps/sessions

### Option B: RSR Only (Drive to Nürburg independently)
1. Fly into CGN (closer) or FRA
2. Take a taxi/shuttle to Nürburg (~€100-150 from CGN)
3. Use RSR for everything — track laps + road tours
4. RSR offers **guided road tours** in supercars (lower excess: €5,000-€12,500)

### Option C: Supercar Road Rental + Track Rental
1. Rent a Lamborghini/Ferrari from specialty company for road cruising (~€1,000-2,500/day)
2. Book separate RSR track session at the Ring
3. Most expensive option but maximum experience

## 9. Practical Notes

- **Touristenfahrten (Tourist Driving):** Open to public on certain days/times. You drive the Nordschleife as a "public road" — speed limit: none, but traffic rules apply. Toll: ~€30 per lap at the gate.
- **Trackdays:** Private events, fewer cars, more organized. RSR offers Premium Trackdays (entry ~€1,399 for Nordschleife).
- **Season:** Nordschleife TF typically runs March-November. Check calendar at https://rsrbooking.com/calendar
- **Getting there:** CGN → Nürburg ~90 min; FRA → Nürburg ~2 hours
- **Hotels near Ring:** Lindner Hotel, Dorint Am Nürburgring, Hotel am Tiergarten, many guesthouses in Nürburg/Adenau
- **Driving license:** Valid EU/international license required. Minimum age varies by car (18-25).
