## 7. APPROACH RECOMMENDATIONS

### For Peder Olofsson (Primary Target):
- His group is literally named "Cardiovascular Inflammation — Neural Control of Inflammation"
- He has clinical experience with VNS for Crohn's disease
- He sits at the exact intersection of electrical stimulation and immune modulation
- Published editorial: "Bioelectronic medicine: an unexpected path to new therapies" (J Internal Med, 2019)
- His work shows electrical signals can modulate specific immune pathways — directly relevant to bio-resonance claims

### Framing the Outreach:
- Lead with scientific curiosity, not belief-based claims
- Frame as: "practitioner achieving reproducible clinical outcomes through electrical/electromagnetic methods that may relate to known neuroimmune pathways"
- Reference the inflammatory reflex / cholinergic anti-inflammatory pathway as possible mechanism
- Propose observational study or case documentation
- Emphasize urgency (practitioner's Alzheimer's diagnosis)
- Offer to fund research/provide access to practitioner

### What Makes This Credible to Academics:
- The inflammatory reflex IS proven science (Nature, Science publications)
- VNS IS FDA-approved for rheumatoid arthritis (2025)
- The vagus nerve DOES modulate immune responses
- Electrodermal activity DOES correlate with autonomic nervous system function
- The gap is: can lower-energy, non-invasive electrical approaches achieve similar results?
