## 1. Introduction

Sacpro Group and Picote are two of the most established names in the European pipe rehabilitation industry. Between us, we cover the full spectrum of what the market needs -- from preparation and cleaning tools to CIPP lining systems, UV curing technology, coatings, and consumables.

Today, contractors across Scandinavia source their materials from multiple suppliers, often without access to local stock, hands-on product demonstrations, or a single point of contact for the full workflow. This creates friction -- slower purchasing decisions, longer lead times, and fragmented supplier relationships.

This proposal outlines a cooperation that eliminates that friction and creates significant value for both companies.

### Why this makes sense

**For Picote**, this cooperation opens a physical retail and distribution channel across Scandinavia that does not exist today. Sacpro Group operates physical stores with dedicated staff, local stock, and direct relationships with contractors, consultants, and facility owners. Picote products would move from being available primarily through online or indirect channels to being showcased, stocked, and sold in professional environments where buying decisions happen face-to-face.

**For Sacpro Group**, Picote's tools and machines are the market standard for pipe cleaning, cutting, and reinstatement work. Offering the full Picote range alongside our own lining systems and consumables means our stores become the one-stop destination for pipe rehabilitation professionals. Contractors walk in for a liner, walk out with everything they need for the full job.

**For the market**, this partnership means faster access to products, better technical support, and a more professional buying experience. Two industry leaders working together rather than in parallel.

### Training centre exposure

Sacpro Group operates two training centres -- in **Falun** and **Malmo** -- where we train relining companies, consultants, and facility owners on installation techniques, product selection, and best practices. As part of this cooperation, the full Picote line of tools and machines will be integrated into our training programmes. Every professional trained at our centres will work with Picote products hands-on, creating direct product familiarity that translates into purchasing decisions. This is sustained, high-value exposure to exactly the right audience -- the people who specify and use these products daily.

## 3. What Sacpro Group expects in return

### 3.1 From Picote Solutions

**A. Junction liner supply agreement**

Picote Solutions purchases junction liners from Sacpro Group, subject to the product meeting Picote's quality expectations.

- Target price: **~EUR 18** per junction liner, size 100-100-45
- Full pricing across sizes detailed in **Appendix A -- Junction Liner Quote**
- Quality validation: Picote evaluates the product and confirms it meets their standards before committing to ongoing supply

**B. Termination of Reliningsvaruhuset contract**

As a condition of this cooperation, Picote Solutions terminates its existing supply/distribution contract with Reliningsvaruhuset. This ensures a clean, exclusive partnership framework without conflicting channel interests in Scandinavia.

**C. Scandinavian distribution rights**

Sacpro Group receives the rights to sell Picote materials and millers across all of Scandinavia (Sweden, Norway, Denmark, Finland). This includes:

- Tools, chains, cutters, and consumables
- Millers and machines
- Coating systems and related products
- Right to stock, market, and distribute through our store network and sales channels

### 3.2 From Picote OY

**A. Liner and epoxy adoption**

Picote OY's installation teams begin using Sacpro Group liner and epoxy for their relining operations, subject to the products performing equal to or better than their current materials.

- Target price after discount: **~EUR 14** per liner, size 100-150
- Full pricing across sizes and products detailed in **Appendix B -- Liner & Epoxy Quote**
- Performance validation: Picote OY evaluates the liner and epoxy under real installation conditions before committing

## 4. Open discussion points

The following items require joint discussion and agreement before finalising the cooperation:

### 4.1 Overlapping product lines

Both Sacpro Group and Picote currently produce and sell:

- **Junction liner tools**
- **Inversion drums**

This overlap creates potential market confusion. We propose discussing the following options:

1. **One party ceases production** of the overlapping products, with the other becoming the sole supplier
2. **Both parties continue** producing and selling, with clear market segmentation or mutual acceptance of the overlap
3. **Joint product development** -- combine expertise into a single, superior product line

A clear resolution on this point strengthens the partnership and avoids channel conflict.
