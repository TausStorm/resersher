## DRIVING / CARS (book through RSR or independently)

### RSR Nurburg -- Track Car Rental
- **What:** Rent prepped track cars for the Nordschleife during Touristenfahrten. Hot hatches to Porsche GT3 RS.
- **Where:** Antoniusweg 1a, 53520 Nurburg (2 min from Ring)
- **Book:** rsrbooking.com or email contact via rsrnurburg.com
- **Price:** From ~200 EUR (2 laps hot hatch) to 3,800 EUR (4 laps GT3 RS 992)
- **Note:** You already have the Lambo Huracan -- but RSR has track-prepped cars with instructors which is a different experience entirely.

### RSR Taxi Laps
- **What:** Pro driver takes you for a hot lap in a race-prepped car. You sit passenger.
- **Book:** rsrnurburg.com
- **Price:** Included in some rental packages or bookable separately

### Hotrod City Tour
- **What:** Drive hotrods through Nurburg village or Koblenz
- **Where:** Eifeldorf Grune Holle, 53520 Nurburg (5 min from Ring) OR Koblenz (1h)
- **Phone:** +49 172 4521698
- **Website:** hotrod-citytour-koblenz.com
- **Fun factor:** High -- ridiculous looking cars, great photo ops

## CASTLES

### Burg Eltz (Castle Tours)
- **What:** One of Germany's most famous medieval castles. Perfectly preserved, stunning setting in a forest valley. Featured on the old 500 DM banknote.
- **Where:** Wierschem (50 min from Ring)
- **Phone:** +49 2672 95050-0
- **Website:** burg-eltz.de
- **Price:** ~12 EUR/person
- **Note:** Genuinely spectacular. Worth the drive.

### Reichsburg Cochem (Castle Tours)
- **What:** Imperial castle overlooking the Moselle river. Wine tasting in the area.
- **Where:** Cochem (45 min from Ring)
- **Phone:** +49 2671 255
- **Website:** burg-cochem.de

### Nurburg Castle
- **What:** Ruins of the castle that gave the Nurburgring its name. Free to walk up, great views over the track.
- **Where:** Nurburg village (5 min walk from Ring)
- **Price:** Free
- **Note:** Quick 30-min activity, great photos

## MOSEL WINE REGION (40-50 min from Ring)

The Moselle valley is one of Germany's best wine regions and it's right next door.

- Drive to **Cochem** (45 min) -- stunning river town, castle, wine tastings
- **Cochem wine tastings** -- walk into any Weingut on the riverfront
- Combine with Reichsburg Cochem castle visit
- Beautiful scenic drive along the Moselle river

---

## KOBLENZ (50 min from Ring)

Bigger city if you want more action:
- **Deutsches Eck** -- where Rhine meets Moselle, iconic landmark
- **Ehrenbreitstein Fortress** -- cable car across the Rhine, massive fortress with views
- **Old town** -- bars, restaurants, shopping
- Good for an evening out if you want city vibes
