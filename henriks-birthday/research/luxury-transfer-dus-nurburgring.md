# Luxury Transfer: Dusseldorf Airport (DUS) to Nurburgring

**Scenario:** 4 guys, 40th birthday trip, late June 2026
**Route:** DUS Airport -> Nurburgring/Nurburg (~130 km, 1.5h drive)
**Need:** Something SPECIAL, not a regular taxi

## 4. SUPERCAR CONVOY WITH DRIVER

This is a niche service. Most supercar experiences in Germany are self-drive rentals, not chauffeured transfers. However, some options exist:

### a) Supercar Experience Companies

| Company | Website | What they offer |
|---------|---------|----------------|
| **Wheelsandmore** | https://tuning-wheels-exhaust.com/ | Baesweiler (near Aachen) -- tuning company, not transfers. But close to the route |
| **Apex Luxury Car Hire** | Search locally | Some offer chauffeur options |
| **Sportwagen Center Rheinland** | Search locally | Supercar rental NRW |

**Reality check:** Getting 4 people in supercars with drivers to Nurburgring is logistically complex and very expensive. Most supercars only seat 2 (driver + 1 passenger), so you'd need 4 cars + 4 drivers. Expect ~2,000-5,000 EUR for a convoy.

**Better alternative:** Rent 1-2 supercars at Nurburgring for track laps (the Nurburgring offers this directly -- see below), and use a luxury van for the actual transfer.

### b) Nurburgring's Own Driving Experiences
- **Website:** https://nuerburgring.de/driving/
- **Co-Pilot Ride:** Mercedes-AMG GT 63 PRO -- ride shotgun with a pro driver on the Nordschleife
- **Formula Training:** Drive a formula car
- **Drift Taxi:** Get sideways with a pro
- **Sports Driver Training:** Various programs
- **Contact:** +49 (0) 2691 302 630

**Recommendation:** Book a luxury transfer TO the Ring, then do Nurburgring's own driving experiences once there. Much better value and more fun.

## 5. HELICOPTER TRANSFER

**The ultimate "special" option.** DUS to Nurburgring by helicopter: ~30-40 minutes vs 1.5h by car.

### a) HELI-FLIGHT JLR gGmbH
- **Website:** https://www.heli-flight.de/en/
- **Base:** Reichelsheim (near Frankfurt) -- but they operate across Germany
- **Services:** Charter & VIP flights, door-to-door helicopter service
- **Fleet:**
  - **Robinson R44:** 3 passengers, 210 km/h -- budget option
  - **Robinson R66:** 4 passengers, 220 km/h -- fits all 4 of you
  - **Airbus H145 D3:** Up to 8 passengers -- luxury option
- **Pricing (indicative, based on their Frankfurt reference prices):**
  - R44: Frankfurt-Koln ~2,000 EUR. DUS-Nurburgring similar distance, estimate **~2,000-3,000 EUR**
  - R66: Slightly more, estimate **~2,500-3,500 EUR**
  - Larger helicopter: **~4,000-6,000 EUR**
- **Extras:** In-flight catering available (they have a Bordkuche/galley service)
- **Can land at Nurburgring?** Would need to arrange a landing site near the circuit
- **Contact:** +49 (0)6035 91000, zentrale@heli-flight.de
- **Available 7 days/week, can depart within 1 hour**

### b) Other Helicopter Charter Options

| Company | Base | Notes |
|---------|------|-------|
| **Air Lloyd** | Bonn/Cologne area | Helicopter charter, closer to DUS |
| **HeliService International** | Emden | Larger operations |
| **Helicopter Travel Munich (HTM)** | Munich | Germany-wide service |
| **RheinHeliCopter** | Search locally | Rheinland-based |
| **Rotorflug** | Friedrichsdorf | Near Frankfurt, charter flights |

**Pricing estimate for DUS -> Nurburgring (4 pax):**
- **Light helicopter (R44/R66):** ~2,000-3,500 EUR
- **Medium helicopter (EC135/H145):** ~4,000-7,000 EUR
- **Luxury helicopter:** ~5,000-10,000+ EUR

**Key considerations:**
- Need to arrange landing permissions at/near Nurburgring
- Weather-dependent (June usually good)
- Luggage limitations
- Need ground transport from helipad to hotel
- Split ~625-1,750 EUR per person (4 people) -- remarkable value for the experience

**Verdict:** At ~750-1,000 EUR per person for a helicopter, this is genuinely worth considering. It's the ultimate 40th birthday flex -- arrive at the Nurburgring by helicopter.
