## 2. FLIGHTS — SAS via Düsseldorf (DUS)

**Why Düsseldorf over Frankfurt:**
- Nearly **half the price** (€102 vs €199 one-way in July)
- Same drive time to Nürburgring (~1.5h)
- Smaller airport = faster in/out

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Route** | CPH → DUS (direct), ~1h 20min |
| **Outbound** | Tue June 30 |
| **Return** | Thu July 2 or Fri July 3 |
| **Estimated cost** | **€800–1,280 total** (4 pax round-trip, economy) |
| **SAS Plus upgrade** | ~2x price, gets you SAS Lounge + Fast Track + food + 2x23kg bags |
| **Book** | flysas.com |

> **Note:** If you're doing the Royal Lounge at CPH, you already get the VIP treatment. SAS Plus mainly adds the lounge on the return at DUS + extra bags. Consider SAS Go Smart as the sweet spot.

## 3. ACCOMMODATION — Three Options

### Option A: Nürburgring Villa Park ⭐ RECOMMENDED
- Literally AT the circuit. Holiday homes inside the Nürburgring complex.
- **Villa5 Classic** — 85 sqm, sleeps 5, fully equipped
- **On-site:** Spa (Vulkaneifel Spa), restaurants, bowling, go-kart track
- **Spa packages:** "Pampering for Two" — €129/pp (private sauna + massage + whirlpool + sparkling wine). Book for 4 = morning sorted.
- https://nuerburgringhotels.de/en/hotels-villa-park/villa-park/holiday-homes
- **Downside:** Zero nightlife. Need to drive to Cologne/Koblenz for a big night.

### Option B: LuxApart Eifel (Luxury)
- High-end apartments near the Ring. "LuxApart Vista" = 135 sqm, 2 bed/2 bath, **private outdoor sauna**, huge terrace.
- Contact: +49 170 270 1707
- https://www.luxapart-eifel.de/en/rates-booking

### Option C: Airbnb in Adenau / Nürburg
- Lots of race-themed rentals, some with views of the track
- Search: https://www.airbnb.com/nurburg-germany/stays/houses
- Also check: https://www.vrbo.com/vacation-rentals/germany/nuerburg/nuerburgring
- **Tip:** Look for places with a hot tub or sauna — common in the area

### Option D: Stay near Cologne (if nightlife is priority)
- 1h drive to track but gives you access to Germany's best nightlife
- Could do one night in Cologne, two nights at the Ring

## 4. RENTAL CAR

### For transport: Normal rental from DUS airport
- SIXT, Hertz, Europcar all at DUS
- BMW 3-series / Mercedes C-class / Audi A4 — from ~€50-80/day
- **SAS has a Hertz partnership** — double EuroBonus points
- **NO rental car from airport is allowed on the track** (insurance void)

### For the track: RSR Nürburg ⭐
The only serious option for track-eligible rental. 25+ years at the Ring, ~100 cars.

**RSR Nürburg** is the main provider for Lamborghini Huracan drives on the Nordschleife. Their website (rsr-nurburg.com) needs to be contacted directly for Lambo availability and pricing — expect **€1,500–2,500** for a multi-lap package with a driving coach.

## 6. ACTIVITIES — FILLING THE DAY

### Morning Spa/Massage (Day 2 or 3)

**If staying at Villa Park:**
- **Vulkaneifel Spa** on-site
- Massages: €42–89 per person
- **"Pampering for Two" package: €129/pp** — private sauna + massage + whirlpool + sparkling wine
- Book 2x "Pampering for Two" = all 4 sorted. ~€516 total.

**Otherwise:**
- **Ahr-Thermen** in Bad Neuenahr-Ahrweiler (30 min drive) — thermal baths + massage
- Mobile massage services available in the area — search "mobile Massage Eifel"

### Go-Karts
- **Ring Kartbahn** — indoor electric go-karts at the Nürburgring
- €15/10 min, walk-in, no booking needed
- Great warm-up before hitting the real track

### Other Ideas
- **Ring Bowling** — at the Nürburgring complex
- **Esports Bar** — racing simulators at the Ring
- **Ahr Valley wine tour** — 30 min drive, beautiful medieval town, amazing red wines
- **Shooting range** — several in the Eifel region

## 7. NIGHTLIFE & ENTERTAINMENT

### At/Near the Ring
- Adenau (10 min) — small-town bars, Blaue Ecke restaurant
- Esports Bar + casual bars at the Ring complex
- Honestly: pretty dead for nightlife

### Koblenz (45 min)
- Altstadt bar district along the Rhine
- Decent selection of bars and restaurants
- More chill vibes

### Cologne (1 hour) ⭐ THE MOVE FOR ONE BIG NIGHT
- **Kölsch beer halls** — Früh, Gaffel, Peters — iconic experience
- **Belgisches Viertel** — trendy bars, cocktail spots
- **Bootshaus** — one of Europe's top clubs
- **Zülpicher Straße** — party street

### Adult Entertainment
Germany is fully legal and regulated. Cologne has the most options:
- FKK clubs, agencies, private bookings
- Mobile/private services available to your accommodation via agencies covering Eifel/Rheinland area
- Ask your accommodation host or search locally — very straightforward in Germany
