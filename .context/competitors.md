# Competitive Landscape

## UVRelining / Sacpro Competitors

### Boldan / Trelleborg

- **Market:** Sweden (Sacpro's home market)
- **Threat:** Winning Sacpro customers on price
- **Context:** Sacpro's high pricing is pushing Swedish customers to Boldan/Trelleborg. Direct customer loss on home turf.

### Linervent

- **Market:** Scandinavia, liner supply
- **Threat:** Channel conflict — sells directly to Sacpro's customers/resellers at same price Sacpro buys for. Also launching own felt liner that could crash Scandinavian liner prices.
- **Context:** See `linervent-strategic-analysis.md` for full analysis.

### General Pricing Pressure

- Competitors producing own liners → lower costs → lower prices
- Sacpro relies on third-party liners (Linervent WO) or own product (FLEX) that is 18 months delayed
- International resellers (Pipe Core, Robocana, S1E) have shown what competitors charge — Sacpro significantly above market

## Researcher Competitors

No direct competitors identified. This is a gap in the market.

### Market Gap

Existing tools assume technical knowledge. Researcher is built specifically for non-technical users who need to understand technical documents.

### Positioning

- **Not** a technical tool for technical people
- **Is** a translation layer - complex → simple
- Target users: people who need to understand, not experts who already do
