# Company

Building Researcher - a tool that simplifies complex documents and standards.

## Basics

- **Name:** Researcher
- **Industry:** Knowledge tools / Documentation
- **Stage:** Building
- **Team:** Solo founder

## Story

Founder works in a role bridging technical engineers and non-technical stakeholders. Constantly sees things overcomplicated when they don't need to be. Built Researcher to solve this - make complex rules, laws, standards, and applications understandable to people without technical backgrounds.

## Founder Profile

- Limited technical know-how (not a developer)
- Strong domain insight from daily contact with technical engineers
- Sees the problem firsthand - complexity that doesn't need to exist
- Building to solve own pain point

## Mission

Make complex documents accessible. Take dense standards, certifications, technical sheets, and norms - explain them simply, show how they apply to specific situations.
